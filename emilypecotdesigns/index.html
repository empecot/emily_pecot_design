Beautiful, Elegant, Smart

(my spaces, that is...)

Your home should reflect your personality, your aspirations and your needs no matter your means. It should be your place of respite after a long day at work and the one place where you feel like yourself.

After years of creating beautiful homes for myself and my loved ones I finally decided to use my keen eye for space and color to provide a service that I love to do.

Splitting my time between my full time home in Jersey City, New Jersey and my family retreat in the Hudson Valley, I have been able to hone my creative abilities when it comes to interior design and expand my horizons professionally.

I look forward to revealing your inner self through a beautiful and attainable space!

-emily
